Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our window tinting services in Boca Raton and throughout Florida.

What is solar window film and how does it benefit me?

Solar window film is a thin professional coating applied to your glass that blocks up to 99% of UV rays, reduces solar heat gain and cuts glare — all while keeping your view clear. It saves you money immediately on air conditioning costs and protects your furniture, flooring and artwork from fading caused by sun exposure.

Will window tinting affect my view or natural light?

No — you'll be surprised at how your view remains completely unaffected! Modern solar films are designed to be nearly invisible while still providing maximum UV and heat protection. We offer a range of tint levels so you can choose exactly how much privacy and solar protection you want.

Do you install one-way mirror film for offices and homes?

Yes! One-way mirror film is one of our specialties. It allows you to see out clearly during the day while preventing outsiders from seeing in — perfect for offices, retail stores, business locations and residential windows facing busy streets. The coating can also be applied to glass surfaces throughout your home.
